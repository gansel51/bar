Persian Garden

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 oz Campari
  • 1 oz St. Germain
  • 1 oz Pomegranate juice
  • 1/4 oz Grenadine
  • 3 oz Prosecco
  • 1-2 oz Soda water
  • Lemon twist

A more complex take on the elderflower spritz ‚Äî Campari adds bitterness and depth, grenadine deepens the sweetness, and fresh pomegranate keeps it bright. Built over ice in a wine glass.

Foreign Lands

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz Lustau East India Sherry
  • 3/4 oz Campari
  • 3/4 oz Pomegranate juice
  • 2 dashes Orange/golden bitters

A low-ABV Negroni riff built on sherry instead of gin. Campari's bitterness plays against the nutty richness of Lustau East India, with pomegranate in place of sweet vermouth. Stirred, not shaken.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Rye whiskey
  • 3/4 oz Lemon juice

  • Angostura bitters
  • Maraschino cherry

A whiskey sour that swaps simple syrup for grenadine ‚Äî the name comes from the French word for pomegranate, grenade. Rye's spice cuts through the sweetness for more backbone.

Silk Road

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 3/4 oz Lemon juice
  • 1/2 oz Honey syrup
  • Pomegranate juice (drizzle)
  • Grenadine (float)
  • Golden bitters

A pomegranate riff on the classic Bramble. Gin and honey syrup provide a floral, herbal base, while the drizzled pomegranate seeps through the crushed ice as you drink ‚Äî jewel-toned and layered.

Persephone

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 oz Blanco tequila
  • 3/4 oz St. Germain
  • 1/2 oz Pomegranate juice
  • 1/2 oz Lime juice
  • Dash orange bitters

Named for the goddess who traded the eternal spring for six pomegranate seeds. Tequila's vegetal brightness meets elderflower sweetness and the tart pull of pomegranate ‚Äî two worlds in one glass.
